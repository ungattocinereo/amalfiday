Block 1 — First Meeting at Amalfi Cathedral

That morning started before sunrise — the quietest time in Amalfi. No traffic, no beach activity, no crowds. Only soft sea light and empty streets.

We met for the first time on the staircase of the Amalfi Cathedral. It was simply the easiest and most recognizable meeting point in town. At that moment, the plan was clear: a private individual photoshoot with classic Amalfi atmosphere and architectural background.

It looked like a standard session — travel portraits, lifestyle content, social media photos. Nothing suggested how strong the final result would be.

Block 2 — Sunrise Photoshoot in Amalfi Harbor

After the meeting, we moved to the harbor area — the pier and the beach — while the location was still completely empty.

Sunrise in the port creates conditions that are impossible later in the day:
	•	Soft reflected light from the sea
	•	Clean backgrounds without tourists
	•	Calm water surface for natural reflections
	•	Full freedom to move between pier, sand, and shoreline

Very quickly it became clear this was not a typical travel shoot. Shiva worked with full professional model control — precise posing, confident movement, and strong understanding of light.

The session naturally evolved into two visual directions:
	•	Morning editorial fashion in natural Amalfi light
	•	Swimwear and coastal lifestyle imagery

By the end of the session, more than 1000 production-quality photos were created.
