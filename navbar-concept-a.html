Logo-First Pill

The navbar is white over this orange gradient hero (in light theme). Scroll down to see it switch to normal. Try the theme toggle — dark theme stays unchanged.

What's new in this version

On-hero mode: When at the top of the page (not scrolled), the navbar detects the hero section and switches all content to white — logo, links, CTA border, social icons, even the theme toggle track. This only happens in light theme; dark theme stays as-is.

Social icons: Instagram & Facebook sit neatly between the theme toggle and Contact button on desktop. Subtle hover circles and a thin divider keep things clean.

On-Hero

White navbar content over dark/gradient heroes. Logo swaps to light variant. Reverts on scroll.

Social

Instagram + Facebook icons with hover effect, separated from CTA by a thin divider.

Theme-safe

On-hero only activates in light theme. Dark theme preserves its own styling untouched.
